Who We Are

Book Stop is a young and scrappy book categorization and search service. Whose sole goal is to provide quality of life improvements to help you find that book, offering search features/filtering miles ahead of Amazon, Good Reads, or Barnes and Nobles.

What you can do help

We are a young compnay and offer all of our services for free. You can help us by donating a little bit of your time to help categorize books, based off genre, and content.

Message From Founder

When I was a youbng boy, my father read the Hobbit to me as a bed time story. Which sparked in me a love of good books that persists to this day.

Thanks to the self publishing boom, it has never been easier to find books. But after spending an hour looking thru kindles book list. Trying to find new authors to follow. I discovered something, while there are now more books being published each year than ever before. It has become drastically harder to find a book worth reading.

Websites like Goodreads, Amazon, Barnes and Nobles do not offer you the saercher any sort of effective filtering system.

My hope is that you too find this helpful to find the pearls from the mud.
Garret R Gang
